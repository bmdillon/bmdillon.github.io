## NIMathsFest 2026 - Jan 22nd 2026
Only the second year running for the NIMathsFest, but a massive participation with approx 500 primary and secondary school students on each day of the festival.
Myself and James from UU were showing how to encode messages with cipher wheels, a very early precursor to modern cryptography.
