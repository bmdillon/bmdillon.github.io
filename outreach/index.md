## NIMathsFest 2026 - Jan 22nd 2026
Only the second year running for the <a href="https://nimathsfest.co.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">NIMathsFest</a>, but a massive participation with approx 500 primary and secondary school students on each day of the festival at the Foyle Arena in Derry.
Myself and <a href="https://www.jajm.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">James</a> from UU were showing how to encode messages with <a href="https://www.sciencemuseumgroup.org.uk/learning/resources/cipher-wheel/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">cipher wheels</a>, a very early precursor to modern cryptography.

## Widening Access and Participation - Jan 20th 2026
Another great event hosted by <a href="https://www.ulster.ac.uk/flexible-education/widening-access-and-participation" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Widening Access and Participation</a> at the Magee campus with over 30 students from schools in Derry and Belfast coming to hear about our new degree course in <a href="https://www.ulster.ac.uk/courses/202627/computing-with-applied-mathematics-42306" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Computing with Applied Mathematics</a>.

## Widening Access and Participation - Oct 23rd 2025

The <a href="https://www.ulster.ac.uk/flexible-education/widening-access-and-participation" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Widening Access and Participation</a> team at Ulster University organised a great event with over 50 school students from around Derry coming together for a tour of our campus, our computing and engineering facilities, and to hear about the benefits of studying STEM subjects at A-level and at university-level.
